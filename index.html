CodeClash

Programming event to check the problem solving skills. There will be algorithmic problems based on the data structures and algorithms.

  • Date: 23 April, 2022
  • Mode: Online

Amaze

Amaze event is exclusively designed for first-year students. The event is planned in such a way that participants will get to learn, improve and grow, which will help them in their further journey.

  • Date: 23 April, 2022
  • Mode: Online

Technovation

Idea presentation event. Participants have to present their innovative project along with the work they have done on implementing the project idea. The project should belong to the Electronics domain.

  • Date: 23 April, 2022
  • Mode: Online

Bussinora

Businorra is 2 day business management event. This event consists of getting knowledge about different innovative products from financial perspective rather than technological views. This event will help the participants when they will become a budding entrepreneur.

  • Date: 23 and 24 April, 2022
  • Mode: Online

Circuitricks

Technical event to check basic knowledge regarding circuits through application and to enhance technical skills of budding technocrats through problem solving

  • Date: 24 April, 2022
  • Mode: Online

Enigma

It is a mystery event. Time to bring out the detective in you, solve numerous hurdles and fight for the ultimate treasure.

  • Date: 24 April, 2022
  • Mode: Online

Xpressions

This event is paper presentation event. This event will provide the platform to the participants to enhance their presentation abilities and help them to excel in the soft skills

  • Date: 24 April, 2022
  • Mode: Online
