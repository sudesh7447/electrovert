BUSINORRA

Learn Big To Earn Big

23 April, 2022

Businorra is 2 day business management event. This event consists of getting knowledge about different innovative products from financial perspective rather than technological views. This event will help the participants when they will become a budding entrepreneur.

Round 1:
The objective test based on the core business management skills and different case studies. Will be conducted 3 days before event

Round 2:
Group Activity. Teams will be formed after round 1 .Teams are expected to give a thorough report and presentation about the task given to the team

Round 3:
Individual Round. The Real life business game will be played among participants to check their spontaneous decisions.
